Five-Strand Coverage Statement:
Each Project Explore lesson package provides documented coverage across all five ELA strand categories. Reading Literature and Informational Text are addressed through the animated episode (shared literary text) and an original informational reading passage differentiated across four tiers. Writing is addressed through tier-differentiated written responses ranging from dictated sentences (Tier 1) to structured analytical paragraphs (Tier 4), plus a cumulative portfolio artifact. Speaking & Listening is addressed through a dedicated discussion protocol page in each tier's student pack, including oral retell, structured partner share, accountable talk, and student-generated questioning. Language (Vocabulary) is addressed through a tiered Launch vocabulary sequence using gesture, image, sentence frame, context clue, and morphological analysis progressions across grades K–4. Foundational Skills are addressed explicitly in Tiers 1–2 through syllable awareness, print concepts, phonics, and fluency activities, and through embedded morphological and Greek/Latin root analysis in Tiers 3–4, in alignment with CA CCSS for ELA/Literacy RF standards at each grade band.
