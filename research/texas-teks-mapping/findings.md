## Part 2: §113.49 — Personal Financial Literacy (HB 27 Standalone Course)

### Key Context
This is the **16 Knowledge and Skills statement** course under TAC §113.49. It's the standalone half-credit PFL course required for Texas HS graduation. The existing repo mapping treated Texas as having "10 standards" with a custom organizational framework — this was **not** aligned to §113.49's actual 16 K&S statements.

## Part 3: §113.76 — Personal Financial Literacy and Economics (PFLE Course)

### Key Context
This is the **10 Knowledge and Skills statement** PFLE course under TAC §113.76. It combines economics content with PFL content. The economics standards (d)(1) and (d)(2) are unique to this course and require the economics-focused L-chapters (L-48 through L-55, L-70).

#### (d)(1) Economics — Fundamental Concepts
*"The student understands the fundamental concepts of economics."*
- (A) Scarcity, choice, opportunity costs in decision making
- (B) Interpret production-possibilities curve; apply scarcity/choice/opportunity costs
- (C) Production-possibilities curve as cost-benefit decision making
- (D) Circular flow model (households, firms, governments in resource/product markets)
- (E) How prices/quantities determined by supply and demand
- (F) Interpret supply-and-demand graph (equilibrium, surpluses, shortages)
- (G) Non-price determinants of supply and demand
- (H) Supply and demand in resource and product markets
