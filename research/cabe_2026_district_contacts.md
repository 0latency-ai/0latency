## Conference Overview

CABE 2026 featured hundreds of workshops on biliteracy, ELD, culturally sustaining pedagogies, and equity. Key events included:
- **Administrative Leadership Symposium (ALS)** — March 6, targeting superintendents/board members on "Multilingual Education in the Current Political and Fiscal Climate"
- **School Site Visits** — SFUSD schools (San Francisco International, Galileo, Abraham Lincoln, Thurgood Marshall, Mission)
- **Two-Day Intensive Institutes** — ELD instruction, Project GLAD®, Spanish immersion literacy
- **High School Student Leadership Academy**
- **Exhibit Hall** with ~200+ exhibitors
