FAQ

What counts as a "memory"?

Each extracted fact, preference, or relationship from a conversation turn is one memory. A single conversation turn typically produces 1–5 memories.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade instantly, downgrade at end of billing period. No contracts, cancel anytime.

What happens if I hit my memory limit?

Extract calls will return a 429 error. Recall continues to work. Upgrade to a higher plan for more capacity.

What's the difference between Pro and Scale?

Pro gives you 100K memories with 25 agents at $19/mo — great for production apps. Scale adds Graph Memory, 1M memories, unlimited agents, priority support with SLA, and advanced cognitive features at $89/mo (64% less than Mem0's comparable tier).

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% with annual billing. Pro drops to ~$15/mo ($182/yr) and Scale to ~$71/mo ($854/yr). Use the billing toggle above to see annual prices.

What is Graph Memory?

Graph Memory maps relationships between memories — people, concepts, events — so your agent understands context, not just keywords. It's available on Scale and Enterprise plans.

What is Negative Recall?

Negative Recall lets your agent know what it should not say or do — corrected facts, retracted preferences, deprecated instructions. Available on all plans.
