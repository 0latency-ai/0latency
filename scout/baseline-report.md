## Competitive Positioning Insights

### What PFL Academy Does That Competitors Don't

1. **ICAP/ILP Integration (Standard 15)** — No competitor has this. Colorado gold.
2. **State-Specific Customization** — 86+ variables, full chapter replacements (not just crosswalks)
3. **3-Tier IEP Scaffolding** — Clean/Guided/Complete, database-backed per student
4. **Implementation Depth** — 62,975-line teacher guide, contextual AI, SME chat
5. **Two-Day Model** — Day 1 (COST framework) + Day 2 (Learning Lab) — structured pedagogy
6. **Platform Features** — Progress tracking, auto-grading, Canvas/Schoology passback, SSO

### What Competitors Do Better

1. **Price** — Free beats $16-20/student every time (unless value is proven)
2. **Market Share** — NGPF's 140K teachers = social proof, network effects
3. **Brand Recognition** — NGPF is THE name in PFL education
4. **Community** — NGPF has conferences, PD, testimonials (cult-like teacher loyalty)

### Strategic Implications

**Don't compete on content volume.** NGPF will always have more teachers contributing free resources.

**Compete on implementation.** Position PFL Academy as the **professional platform** for districts serious about compliance, scaffolding, and measurable outcomes.

**Target:** Districts with budget + mandate pressure (Colorado ICAP, Kentucky 1.0 credit, Texas HB 27). Not individual teachers looking for freebies.
