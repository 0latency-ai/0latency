## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

CABE 2026 already happened (March 4-7). This report covers what's extractable from public sources. The **full exhibitor directory** and **complete session program** were hosted on Whova (conference app) which blocks web scraping. What we have: all confirmed sponsors (16 companies), keynote/featured speakers (25+), planning committee co-sponsor districts (9), and partner organizations (23).

**Key finding:** 4 direct competitors to Project Explore were exhibiting. Several high-value distribution partners and district warm leads identified.

**Recommendation:** Request the full exhibitor list from CABE directly (info@gocabe.org) or check if Justin has Whova access from registration. The 16 sponsors represent maybe 10-15% of total exhibitors.
